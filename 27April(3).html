Batting Skills

Learn the techniques of modern cricket batting, including proper stance, grip, and shot selection. Understand how to play both defensive and attacking shots with confidence. Improve timing, footwork, and hand-eye coordination for better performance. Practice handling different types of bowlers like pace and spin effectively. Build consistency and confidence to score runs in any match situation.

Bowling Techniques

Explore the fundamentals of fast and spin bowling with proper action and control. Learn how to maintain line and length to challenge batsmen consistently. Develop variations such as swing, seam, yorkers, and spin deliveries. Understand strategies to outsmart batsmen in different match scenarios. Enhance accuracy and stamina to become a reliable bowler for your team.

Fielding & Team Play

Master the importance of fielding with agility, speed, and sharp reflexes. Practice catching, throwing, and ground fielding techniques effectively. Understand field placements and teamwork strategies during matches. Improve communication and coordination with teammates on the field. Build overall fitness and awareness to contribute in every aspect of the game.
